About QuickScoot

Discover the story behind QuickScoot and our mission to revolutionize urban mobility in India. We are committed to providing eco-friendly, convenient, and affordable electric scooter rentals to help you navigate the bustling streets of India with ease.

Our Mission

At QuickScoot, we believe that urban mobility should be sustainable, efficient, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion, lower carbon emissions, and provide a seamless riding experience for our customers. By offering electric scooters that are easy to rent and fun to ride, we aim to transform the way people move around cities.

We are dedicated to creating a greener future by promoting eco-friendly transportation solutions. Every ride with QuickScoot contributes to a cleaner environment and a healthier planet.

Our History

QuickScoot was founded in 2020 with the vision of transforming urban mobility in India. Starting with a small fleet of electric scooters in New Delhi, we have expanded our services to multiple cities across the country. Our journey has been fueled by a passion for sustainability and a commitment to making city travel more efficient and enjoyable.

Over the years, we have partnered with local governments, businesses, and communities to promote eco-friendly transportation. Today, QuickScoot is a trusted name in urban mobility, serving thousands of customers every day.
